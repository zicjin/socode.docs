strrev Function

strrev reverses the characters in a string. Note that the characters are treated as Unicode characters (in technical terms, Unicode grapheme cluster boundaries are respected).

strrev(string)

Examples 

> strrev("hello")
olleh
> strrev("a ☃")
☃ a
