DeviceMotionEventAcceleration: z

The DeviceMotionEventAcceleration.x read-only property indicates the amount of acceleration that occurred along the Z axis in a DeviceMotionEventAcceleration object.

Value

A double indicating the amount of acceleration along the Z axis. See Accelerometer values explained for details.
