DeviceMotionEventRotationRate: alpha

The DeviceMotionEventRotationRate.alpha read-only property indicates the rate of rotation around the Z axis, in degrees per second.

Value

A double indicating the rate of rotation around the Z axis, in degrees per second. See Accelerometer values explained for details.
