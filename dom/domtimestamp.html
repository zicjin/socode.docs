DOMTimeStamp

The DOMTimeStamp type represents an absolute or relative number of milliseconds, depending on the specification in which it appears.

Note: DOMTimeStamp has been removed from the WebIDL specification, but is referenced in other specifications. In the High Resolution Time specification, DOMTimeStamp has been redefined as EpochTimeStamp.
