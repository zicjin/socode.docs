tf.experimental.numpy.prod

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's prod.

tf.experimental.numpy.prod(
    a, axis=None, dtype=None, keepdims=None
)

Unsupported arguments: out, initial, where.

See the NumPy documentation for numpy.prod.
