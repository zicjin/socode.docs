tf.compat.v1.quantize_v2

Please use tf.quantization.quantize instead.

tf.compat.v1.quantize_v2(
    input,
    min_range,
    max_range,
    T,
    mode='MIN_COMBINED',
    name=None,
    round_mode='HALF_AWAY_FROM_ZERO',
    narrow_range=False,
    axis=None,
    ensure_minimum_range=0.01
)
