tf.compat.v1.tpu.XLAOptions

XLA compilation options.

tf.compat.v1.tpu.XLAOptions(

Attributes
use_spmd_for_xla_partitioning Boolean. Whether to use XLA's SPMD partitioner instead of MPMD partitioner when compiler partitioning is requested.
