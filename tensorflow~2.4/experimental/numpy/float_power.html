tf.experimental.numpy.float_power

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's float_power.

tf.experimental.numpy.float_power(
    x1, x2
)

Unsupported arguments: out, where, casting, order, dtype, subok, signature, extobj.

See the NumPy documentation for numpy.float_power.
