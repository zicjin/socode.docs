tf.experimental.numpy.expm1

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's expm1.

tf.experimental.numpy.expm1(
    x
)

Unsupported arguments: out, where, casting, order, dtype, subok, signature, extobj.

See the NumPy documentation for numpy.expm1.
