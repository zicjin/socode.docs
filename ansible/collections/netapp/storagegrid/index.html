  • na_sg_grid_regions – NetApp StorageGRID manage Regions for the grid.
  • na_sg_grid_user – NetApp StorageGRID manage users.
  • na_sg_org_container – Manage buckets on StorageGRID.

  • na_sg_org_info – NetApp StorageGRID Org information gatherer.
